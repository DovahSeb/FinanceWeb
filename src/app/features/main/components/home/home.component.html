This system is developed for the Human Resource Department of the Ministry of Finance, National Planning, and Trade (MoFNPT). It aims to centralize and streamline HR operations by offering secure access to employee data.

Note: The Leave Management Module is currently under development and will be available soon.

Current Capabilities

Employee Records

View and manage active employee records across all departments within MoFNPT.

 Secure Data Access

Only authorized HR staff can access and update employee information.

 User-Friendly Interface

Designed with simplicity and efficiency in mind for a better user experience.
